How does STIBD work?

STIBD provides a powerful processing of several sources of information, in order to extract every concept of interest from those sources. From this, when the student makes his questions, the tutor search between the concepts obtained to give the necessary answers..

What's STIBD purpose?

The STIBD project aims to promote education, making use of today's advanced technology. It also aims to connect students in order to facilitate organizing educational events, study sessions and more.
